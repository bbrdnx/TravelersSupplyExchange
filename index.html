The hero flow

  1. Need it tonight. One clear job: find gear you didn't pack.
  2. Matched nearby. Distance + re-circulation count do the trust work.
  3. Trust signals. "Not for sale" banner, verified owner, circulation history.
  4. Safe exchange. Safety is a designed screen, not fine print.
  5. Close the loop. Returning the item is the rewarded behavior.
This is the MVP cut: one flow that proves the riskiest assumption (find + safely get gear, fast). Offload, item-journey, and achievements are named aloud, not built. Inventory is seeded to dodge the cold-start problem for the demo.
